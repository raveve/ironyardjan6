A Busy Year

2014 started with a trip to Los Angeles to see the Auburn Tigers play for the BCS National Championship. In March a trip to Houston for a friend's wedding. May brought about a trip to New York City for a friend's medical school graduation. June had a quick trip to Greenville to hike in Caesars Head State Park. In August a trip to Montana and Oregon was had. Then the fall held many weekend trips to Auburn for football games.

Although there were many excersions during 2014, only three will be highlighted within their own page. Houston, New York and Montana will be highlighted and can be found in the navigation.

As always, more can be read on all these adventures (and future adventures to come!) on Slightly Askew.
